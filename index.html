Welcome to Ratdom Wayfinder

This product is to be used in conjunction with Andor's Trail Directory map to navigate your way through Ratdom. Just copy the name of your current map to "start" and your destination to "end", and check if some doors in the maze are unlocked, and press the button to see the magic!

Note: some maps are divided into separated "up" and "down" section.

Hint: just find the warden and you might not need to use this website again.
